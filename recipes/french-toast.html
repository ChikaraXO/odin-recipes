French Toast

Description

French Toast is a sweet and easy to make breakfast to start the day on a high note. With only 7 ingredients and about 15 minutes, you can have a restaurant-quality breakfast to start your day.

Note: The recipe will work better with thicker slices to hold the batter we soak it in.

Ingredients

  • 2/3 cup milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • salt (to taste)
  • 6 thick slices of bread
  • unsalted butter
    •

    Steps

    1. Get a shallow mixing bowl, and add in your milk, eggs, vanilla, cinnamon, and salt (a pinch should do). Whisk all the ingredients together.
    2. Start heating your skillet on medium-high heat. Lightly coat it with the unsalted butter.
    3. Dunk your bread into the egg mixture. Soak both sides.
    4. Transfer the slice to the skillet, and cook for about 3-4 minutes per side.
    5. Serve hot. Feel free to add syrup, fruit, powdered sugar, more butter, whatever you like!
