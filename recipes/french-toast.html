French Toast

Description

French Toast is a sweet and easy to make breakfast to start the day on a high note. With only 7 ingredients and about 15 minutes, you can have a restaurant-quality breakfast to start your day. Note: The recipe will work better with thicker slices to hold the batter we soak it in.

Ingredients

  • 2/3 cup milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • salt (to taste)
  • 6 thick slices of bread
  • unsalted butter
